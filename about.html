Dear How To

About Dear How to:

We’re a group of information designers who are always thinking out of the box or… rethinking how to make the box. We love coming up with ways of explaining things such as how to walk against the crowd or how to look awake.

So, inspired by Giorgia Lupi and Stefanie Posavec’s Dear Data project we decided that we could try a similar experiment with our own skills. Each week for one year, each of us draws instructions on a blank postcard and sends it in turn to one or the other.

The only rule is NO TEXT!

Sol, Josefina and Tomoko are designers who met at the MA Information Design at Reading in 2013. Sol is an independent information designer from Mexico City, living in the Alps. Josefina is a French gal from Chile and is currently working on her PhD in Reading, and Tomoko, who joined the project a few weeks in, is from Japan via Lebanon, and works as an information designer in Richmond.


New postcards are posted every Monday.
